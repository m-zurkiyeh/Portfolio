About Me

I am a new graduate that has completed a 3-year program on Computer Engineering Technology - Computing Science and obtained an Ontario College Advanced Diploma for doing so at Algonquin College. My passions are Video Games, my Culture and Programming. During my time at Algonquin, I have studied programming languages such as Java, C, C++, C#, SQL, Assembly and studied various aspects of programming such as Computational Mathematics, Object-Oriented Programming, Data Structures, Real-Time Programming and Interfacing (Working with Arduino)
